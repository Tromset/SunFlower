screen companion · 100% local

Welcome to sunflower.

Hold ⌃ ⌥ anywhere on your Mac to talk. Sunflower listens, sees your screen and answers out loud — the model runs on your machine, through Ollama. Nothing ever leaves home.

step 02 / 03 · permissions

Give it eyes and ears.

Four macOS permissions, nothing more: to listen while you hold ⌃ ⌥, and to look at the screen you show it. Everything stays on your Mac.

microphone to hear your question.
accessibility to catch ⌃ ⌥ everywhere.
screen recording to see what you describe.
screen content to point at the right spot.
step 03 / 03 · the model

The model runs on your machine.

Sunflower talks to Ollama on your Mac and listens with Whisper locally. No key, no account, no remote server.
